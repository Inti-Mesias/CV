Resume

Political scientist and Master in Urban-Regional Planning with experience in research and coordination of analysis processes through technological tools for geographic information processing (QGIS and ArcGis), data analysis (Python and PowerBI) and qualitative analysis (ATLAS.ti) for territorial management, formulation and evaluation of public policies with population and spatial approaches. It is my goal that the research and professional work that I advance contributes to spatial justice and overcoming inequalities.

Experience

- Employment reference for public youth policy. Superior Mayor of Bogota. Implementation and evaluation of the District Youth Public Policy in its employment component, with emphasis on vulnerable population and "Parceros por Bogotá" program. May 2023 – January 2024

- Youth employment and productivity consultant. International Labor Organization - ILO. Methodological design for the collection of inputs through a tripartite sectoral table for the formulation of the National Public Youth Policy, in relation to employment and productivity. August 2023 – September 2023

- Territorial Coordinator. Superior Mayor of Bogota. Coordination of 77 professionals for the implementation and evaluation of the Public Youth Policy in Bogotá. Support to the community, government and third sector for the implementation of the Youth Citizenship Statute. February 2020 – January 2023

- Youth participation consultant. International Republican Institute Methodology design and its application for the appropriation of the Youth Citizenship Statute by local governments and youth leaders in the national territory, Law 1622 of 2013. April 2022 – June 2022

- Consultant. Organization of Ibero-American States - OEI. Design of a Territorial Management Model for the implementation of public policies regarding infancy, childhood, adolescence and families in Colombia. August 2019 – December 2019

- Social manager. Consultoría Colombiana S.A. Coordinator of the participation component for the formulation of modern POT/POD with emphasis on PDET municipalities within the framework of the peace process in Colombia. September 2018 – December 2018

- Consultant.Congress of Colombia. Professional support to the Legislative Technical Unit (UTL) of Senator Jorge Enrique Robledo Castillo on issues related to youth, territory and citizen participation. November 2016 – February 2018
